Welcome to Page Mail

Page Mail lets you quickly send the current web page by email, including the page title, selected text, and URL.

  • Choose your preferred email service (Email link handler, Gmail® or Outlook®) in Options.
    • For the Email link handler, your system's default mail app or webmail will be used.
      You can test it here, get help here.
    • For Gmail® or Outlook® (supported only on desktop, not Android), you must be logged in to your selected email service in your browser for Page Mail to work properly.
  • Optional: Set a custom Subject prefix for your emails in Options.
  • Remove the extension at any time.
Note: For options, open Settings for Page Mail in the browser Extensions Manager.
